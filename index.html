ABOUT THE COLLEGE

Bhaskaracharya College of Applied Sciences is a constituent college
of the University of Delhi and one of the top ranked colleges in India.
Established in 1995, the college offers undergraduate courses in various disciplines of sciences and
applied sciences. The institution has been honoured with 'Star College' status by the
Department of Biotechnology, Government of India.

The college currently has fifteen departments and offers eleven undergraduate courses in various disciplines of sciences and applied sciences.
